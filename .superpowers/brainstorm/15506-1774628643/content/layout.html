Layout Principles

  • Full-screen Hydra canvas as the primary experience -- visuals are everything
  • Tab key toggles the control panel (slides in from right)
  • Minimal HUD overlay when controls are hidden: BPM, current preset name, audio level
  • Dark, borderless aesthetic -- the UI melts into the visuals
  • Three panel sections: Audio Engine, Visual Engine, Mappings
  • Keyboard always active for playing notes regardless of panel state
